How to Use

  • Build your character — Choose Solo, Couple, or Group at the top of Part 1. Each character gets their own tab, plus a Quick Preset dropdown for instant one-click styling.
  • Set the scene — Complete background, lighting, and pose details in Part 2.
  • Just want a patch? — The Chenille Patch Builder generates a standalone patch design, no character needed.
  • Want a product photo? — The Product Mockup Generator still needs a character or a patch — pick "Patch Design" as the source to skip the character.
  • Save your shop's look — Set colors, art style, and tagline once in the Brand Board and apply them anytime to a character or patch. Or click "Generate Brand Board Graphic" for a standalone style-guide image (logo, colors, fonts, and mockups) — that one needs nothing but a Brand Name.
  • Finish up — Click Generate Prompt, then Copy, Save, or create 3 Variations.

Here is how to use The Vibe Vault Studio™ by Sivad Tamara Graphics® in just a few easy steps:

3
Try a Quick PresetEach character tab has a "✨ Quick Preset" dropdown — one click instantly fills outfit, scene, lighting, and style. Your identity choices stay exactly as you set them.
4
Use Randomize for InspirationClick Randomize at the top to instantly fill all fields with a random character build.

Want Just a Patch or Product Mockup?The Chenille Patch Builder can generate a standalone patch design with no character at all. That one only needs a Brand Name, no character or patch required.

Generate Your PromptClick Generate Prompt and your full detailed AI prompt will appear on the right.
